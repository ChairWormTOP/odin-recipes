Chicken Soup

Description

This is a Chicken Soup Recipe. This is delicious to eat in the winter times!

Ingredients

  • Raw Chicken
  • Chicken Broth
  • Potatoes
  • Vegetables Mix

Steps

  1. Boil Raw Chicken
  2. Boil Water with Chicken Broth
  3. Add boiled Chicken
  4. Add Vegetables Mix
  5. Add seasoning for taste
  6. Serve to eat!
